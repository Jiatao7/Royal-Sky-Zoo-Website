Savanna Safari

Open Sunday Only

Description

Are you ready to embark on the adventure of a lifetime? Come experience the wonder and majesty of the African savanna with our unforgettable safari tour. As you journey through our safari zoo, you'll have the opportunity to see some of the world's most incredible wildlife up close and personal. From majestic elephants and towering giraffes, to powerful lions and graceful zebras, you'll witness the natural beauty of Africa in all its glory. Our expert guides will lead you through the savanna, sharing their knowledge and insights about the animals and their habitats. And with our specially designed safari vehicles, you'll be able to see everything from a comfortable and safe distance. So whether you're an avid wildlife enthusiast or just someone looking for a once-in-a-lifetime adventure, our savanna safari is the perfect activity for you.
