Animal Carousel

Open Daily

Whether you're young or young at heart, a ride on our carousel is an experience you won't want to miss. Our beautifully crafted carousel features a variety of animal-themed seats, from majestic horses to playful elephants and everything in between. With its bright colours and whimsical design, our carousel will transport you to a magical world of wonder and delight.

Location: Near Front Entrance

Cost:
$3 per ticket
Free with Zoo Pass

Restrictions:
- Children under 5 years old must ride with an adult (18+)
- Weight limit is 100 kg




Buffalo Bumper Cars

Open Daily

Are you ready for a wild ride? Buckle up and get ready to smash and crash your way to victory on our buffalo bumper cars! With their sturdy frames and rubber bumpers, our bumper cars are designed for maximum fun and excitement. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a first-time rider, you'll love the adrenaline rush that comes with zooming around the track, dodging obstacles and colliding with other cars.

Location: Savanna Region

Cost:
$5 per ticket
Free with Zoo Pass

Restrictions:
- Rider must be at least 5 years old
- Children under 8 years old must ride with an adult (18+)




Soaring Eagle Zipline

Open Weekends

Soar to new heights and experience the thrill of a lifetime on our soaring eagle zipline! Feel the wind rushing through your hair as you zip along our exhilarating course, suspended high above the ground. With stunning views of the surrounding landscape and the rush of adrenaline pumping through your veins, you'll feel alive like never before.

Location: Mountain Region

Cost:
$10 per ticket
Free with Zoo Pass

Restrictions:
- Rider must be at least 8 years old
