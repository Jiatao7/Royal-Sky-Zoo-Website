Treetop Adventure

Open Weekends

Description

Are you ready for a nature adventure like no other? Come explore the treetops with our thrilling treetop trekking experience! Challenge yourself and your friends as you navigate through a series of obstacles high up in the trees, including wobbly bridges, tightropes, and zip lines. With multiple levels of difficulty, our treetop trekking courses offer something for everyone.

Courses

We offer 8 treetop adventure courses at our zoo:

  • Beginner:
    • Canopy Cruiser
    • Jungle Jamboree
    • Branching Out
  • Intermediate:
    • Skyline Safari
    • Timber Trailblazer
    • Wildwood Wayfarer
  • Advanced:
    • Forest Frenzy
    • Aerial Adventure

Safety

- Guests must wear closed-heel shoes to enter
- Guests must wear a climbing harness (will be provided) on the course at all times
- Children under 5 years old must be accompanied by an adult (18+)
