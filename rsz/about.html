About Us

Our Mission

Providing a fun and educational experience for visitors of all ages, while also promoting conservation and awareness of the importance of protecting our planet's wildlife.

Our Story

The Royal Sky Zoo started in 1979 as a small sanctuary for rescued animals. The sanctuary's goal was to create a place where animals could live safely and comfortably, while also educating the public about the importance of animal welfare and conservation.

Over time, the sanctuary grew with more and more animals being brought in. Soon, in 1986, the sanctuary evolved into a full-fledged zoo, with proper habitats and education programs. The zoo became very popular in the community, with visitors coming from all over to see the animals, learn about conservation efforts, and support the zoo's mission.

But despite its growth, the zoo has never lost sight of its original mission. We remain committed to providing the best possible care for our animals, supporting conservation efforts, and educating the public about the importance of protecting our planet's wildlife.
