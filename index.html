Our Zoo

The Royal Sky Zoo is home to a diverse range of animals from all over the world. We strive to create natural habitats for our animals, providing them with the space, food, and enrichment they need to thrive. In addition to our animal exhibits, we offer a variety of educational programs and events for visitors to learn about the animals and their habitats.

Animals

Our zoo is home to over 700 animals from more than 20 different species. From the majestic lions of the savanna to the cute penguins of the tundra, there are so many fascinating creatures for you to come see.
